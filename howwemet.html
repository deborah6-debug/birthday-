how we met 💙

😌 first impression

Girl… let’s talk about my first impression of you.

Honestly, it was very mixed at the beginning. I wasn’t immediately “feeling it” in a deep way. I was just observing everything.

I noticed your love for music though… and that part stood out to me. Because I’m also really into music, so that was one of those quiet similarities I couldn’t ignore.

But at the same time, I was still a bit confused about you. I remember moments where I felt like you were distant or snubbing me. Especially when I even asked you for your note and it didn’t go the way I expected.

I was literally thinking, “who is this girl… why is she acting like this?”

And I won’t lie, I filled in the gaps with my own assumptions at first. I thought you were just uninterested or maybe a bit rude.

But then there were small things I couldn’t ignore… like the earpiece(oraimo) moment 😭💙 I don’t even know why that stuck in my head, but it did.

There was just something about your energy soft, a bit chaotic, a bit distant, but not empty. I didn’t fully understand it then, but I remember thinking: “this person is different… I just don’t know how yet.”

And funny enough… that confusion slowly turned into understanding later on.

😂 the funny part

I remember when I used to knock your head at any given time… and you would still correct me like nothing happened 😭💙

I also remember when I would just blurt out insults randomly and you would still calmly correct me instead of reacting the way other people would have.

And that moment when someone said we looked alike… and both of us just looked at each other like “huh??” 😂

I still remember the day you came to my room to make hair. That was the day you discovered I am actually very loud 😭 And funny enough… you didn't even make your hair again 💀

But after that day, I started coming to your room instead… that your roommates got tired.

And class issues? 😭 Let’s not even lie… the days we had misunderstandings because of all your male friends were something else entirely.

Nna mehhh 😂💙 I actually remember a lot… too much to even write here.

💙 now

And now… when I look at everything in full, it’s kind of funny how it all started.

From confusion… to laughter… to small arguments… to memories I didn’t even realize were becoming important.

We didn’t start perfectly, and maybe that’s what makes it feel real.

Because now, you’re not just someone I remember… you’re someone who became part of my everyday thoughts in ways I didn’t expect.

And yeah… I’m still here, still talking, still laughing at everything we’ve been through.

Somehow… it all makes sense now.

💙 something I’ve thought about

I love you… and I know you do too, in your own way.

There are times I’ve paused and wondered…

Am I doing too much in this friendship? Does she see me the way I see her? Does she love me… or am I just forcing myself into something that isn’t the same on both sides?

But deep down… I still choose to believe in us.

I know you love me, and I will always love you too.

Becoming your friend is honestly one of the best gifts this university has given me.

I still bless the day Eunice told you she could make hair… and you came to my room. That day started something I didn’t even understand then, but now I see how beautiful it became.

And somehow… the rest has just been history for me.
